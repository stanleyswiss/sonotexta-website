Why people switch to Sonotexta

🎙️

Instant Results

Your words appear the moment you speak them. Real-time cloud streaming with sub-second latency. 98+ languages detected automatically — no switching, no configuration.

🌍

Think in One Language, Write in Another

Dictate in your native tongue and Sonotexta delivers the text in any of 24 languages. Per-contact tone profiles mean your wife gets casual Czech while your boss gets formal English.

👤

Smart Contact Detection

Sonotexta reads who you're chatting with in iMessage, Teams, or Slack and automatically applies the right language and tone profile. No manual switching needed.

✨

AI That Polishes Your Words

Choose from 8 built-in enhancement presets — Professional, Bullet Points, Summary, Action Items, and more. Or write your own. Filler words gone, grammar fixed, tone perfected.

🌐

Globe Key & Hold to Talk

Use the Globe key as your dictation trigger. Two modes: tap to start/stop, or hold to talk and release to finish — just like a walkie-talkie. Custom keyboard shortcuts also supported.

🔒

Your Voice, Your Rules

Keep everything on your Mac with three on-device engines. Or use cloud streaming for maximum speed. You choose — no subscriptions required for local processing.

⚡

Invisible Until You Need It

Lives in your menu bar. No dock icon, no windows. A floating pill appears when you dictate, disappears when you're done. Works in every app — Messages, Slack, Obsidian, VS Code.

🔄

Automatic Updates

Always stay on the latest version. Sonotexta checks for updates automatically and installs them with one click — no manual downloads needed.

🧠

OpenAI, Groq (free), Gemini (free), OpenRouter (200+ models), Ollama (fully local & private), and Ollama Cloud. Bring your own key or run completely offline.

Release Notes

v3.16.2 June 14, 2026 Latest
  • Fixes translation profiles that set a specific input language (for example English to German) translating into the wrong language. They now translate into the language you selected.
v3.16.1 May 31, 2026
  • Fixes a crash on macOS 26.3.1 (Tahoe) when starting dictation with any engine — thanks to Blaine for testing the fix end-to-end on his hardware.
  • Fixes Whisper returning empty transcripts on English audio when input language is set to Auto.
  • Updates now always ask before installing — no more silent installs on relaunch.
  • Known limitation on macOS Tahoe: some USB microphones (including some webcam mics) may fail when set as Sonotexta's per-app input override. Workaround: choose your microphone in System Settings → Sound → Input.
v3.16.0 May 26, 2026
  • New "Parakeet Live (English)" engine — fastest offline English dictation with live preview as you speak.
  • Picks between 160ms and 320ms chunk sizes in Settings → Dictation, trading latency for accuracy.
  • Launch-time update popup now appears when an update is available (was silently skipped on 3.15.x).
  • Existing Parakeet TDT v3 multilingual engine unchanged.
v3.15.0 May 22, 2026
  • Redesigned Settings: five clearer tabs — Dictation, Profiles, Connections, AI & Language, About & Stats.
  • New Connections tab puts every API key on one page with inline Test and Remove buttons.
  • System / Light / Dark theme picker in Dictation → Appearance.
  • First-run wizard for new installs; What's-new sheet on first launch for upgraders.
v3.14.3 May 20, 2026
  • DeepL translation support. Add your own DeepL API key in AI Settings for higher-quality translation on any macOS version.
  • Test buttons for every API key — Deepgram, ElevenLabs Scribe, and DeepL now show ✓/✗ inline.
  • Auto-update fixed. Updates now check automatically on every launch.
  • Updates tab shows when the last automatic check ran.
v3.14.2 May 13, 2026
  • Update checks now run every time the app starts. Previously Sonotexta only checked for updates on a timer and could miss a new version until hours after launch.
  • Automatic update checks now happen every 4 hours instead of every 12.
v3.14.1 May 13, 2026
  • Fixed: local Whisper double-paste when AI Post-Processing is off. A timing race in the stop flow caused the same text to land twice. Only affected Whisper with AI cleanup disabled; cloud engines were never affected.
v3.14.0 May 13, 2026
  • Faster paste when stopping with Whisper. Local Whisper transcription no longer re-processes the whole recording on stop — if you pause briefly before pressing stop, the result lands instantly instead of waiting 5–15 seconds.
  • Better translation for Bulgarian, Czech and other non-English source languages. Apple Translation now uses the profile’s actual input language instead of always assuming English. Unsupported language pairs are clearly flagged so you can switch on AI Post-Processing instead.
  • OpenAI Realtime works for Cloud subscribers without their own key. Previously the engine refused to start unless you had pasted your own OpenAI API key, even though your subscription includes it.
  • Translate engine gets a source-language hint. Native translation results are more reliable on short Bulgarian/Czech/Slovak utterances.
  • Local Ollama stays local. Cloud subscribers who pick Ollama for privacy are no longer silently routed through the managed OpenAI proxy.
v3.13.2 May 13, 2026
  • New: ElevenLabs Scribe v2 Realtime. A sixth speech-to-text engine with ~150ms latency, 90+ languages and best-in-class multilingual accuracy. Bring your own ElevenLabs API key under Settings → Engine.
  • Fixed: Bluetooth headsets now work reliably. AirPods Max, AirPods Pro and other Bluetooth headsets no longer stop recording silently a moment after dictation starts.
  • Clearer engine settings. Settings → Engine now shows the active default engine at the top and tags each engine’s configuration as Active or Profile-override only.
v3.13.0 May 11, 2026
  • Better non-English recognition with Deepgram and Whisper. Sonotexta now passes the profile’s input language to both engines — Bulgarian, Russian, Polish and other Cyrillic / Slavic dictations land cleanly instead of being mis-detected.
  • Per-profile engine overrides are usable. Engine Settings now shows every engine’s API key and model picker at all times, so a profile that overrides to a different engine can reach its configuration.
  • Cleanup prompt no longer leaks into your text. Custom cleanup prompts are isolated as a style guide the model is forbidden to quote.
v3.12.0 May 9, 2026
  • Native translation now included with Sonotexta Cloud. No OpenAI API key required for Cloud subscribers.
  • More reliable native translation. Trailing words no longer drop when you stop dictating quickly.
  • Your API key is respected. When you provide your own OpenAI key, Sonotexta uses it — even on a Cloud subscription.
  • Cleaner translated output. Fixes a case where AI cleanup could undo the translation and revert text to the source language.
v3.11.0 May 9, 2026
  • Faster, more reliable native translation. Quicker stop response and a configurable max-wait in Settings → AI.
  • Reliable paste delivery. Fixes the case where transcribed text landed on the clipboard but never reached your app.
  • Vocabulary. Teach the AI cleanup how you pronounce specific terms — useful for product names and homophones.
  • Update prompt comes to the front. Checking for Updates now reliably brings the prompt to the foreground.
v3.6 April 5, 2026
  • Input language per profile. Tell the engine what language you're speaking for better accuracy on short phrases and similar languages.
  • Dictate while on a call. Improved FaceTime and Zoom mic sharing for reliable concurrent mic access.
  • In-app update history. New Updates tab in Settings so you can check release history and install new versions without leaving the app.
  • Fixed AI cleanup treating dictation as instructions. Post-processing now always cleans your text instead of acting on it.
v3.0 April 4, 2026
  • Rebranded to Sonotexta. New name, same app — better foundation for what comes next.
  • Managed subscriptions launched. Sonotexta AI ($7.99/mo) and Cloud ($14.99/mo) bring zero-config AI and cloud transcription — no API keys needed.
  • 8 AI enhancement presets per profile. Clean & Correct, Professional, Bullet Points, Summary, Action Items, and more — or write your own.
  • Redesigned dictation overlay. Live waveform, real-time transcript, profile badge, and delivery confirmation.
v1.8 March 27, 2026
  • Pro tier launched. $9.99 one-time purchase with a 7-day free trial unlocks all BYOK cloud features.
  • 6 AI providers. OpenAI, Groq (free), Gemini (free), OpenRouter (200+ models), Ollama (local), and Ollama Cloud.
  • Redesigned Settings. Sidebar navigation with card-based layout across all sections.
  • Security hardening. API keys stored in Keychain, sanitized logging, and prompt injection protection.
v1.0 March 25, 2026
  • Initial release. Three on-device engines: Apple Speech, OpenAI Whisper (WhisperKit), and NVIDIA Parakeet TDT.
  • Per-app profiles. Set output language, tone, and AI cleanup per application.
  • AI post-processing via BYOK. Connect your own OpenAI key for grammar correction and tone adjustment.
  • Menu bar app. Floating dictation overlay with animated waveform — no dock icon, always ready.
