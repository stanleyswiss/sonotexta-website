Screeny

Capture any area, window or screen — then annotate, blur, stamp and share. A fast, native menu-bar screenshot tool for your Mac.

⬇ Download for macOS

Requires macOS 14+ · Apple Silicon · Signed & notarized · Free

🎯

Precise area capture

Crosshair selection with live dimensions across every display — full screen and window capture too.

✏️

Instant annotation

Arrows, lines, shapes, highlight, text, crop and cut — movable, resizable, with undo/redo.

🌫️

Blur & pixelate

Hide sensitive text with an adjustable Gaussian blur or pixelate. Numbered, check and ✕ stamps.

🖼️

Make it pop

Frame captures on a solid or gradient background with padding and a soft drop shadow.

⌨️

Global shortcuts

Trigger any capture mode system-wide — even the top-left key works on both ANSI and ISO keyboards.

🗂️

Library & auto-update

Browse past captures, copy or clean up, and get new versions automatically.

Install in 3 steps

1

Open the downloaded DMG and drag Screeny into Applications.

2

Launch it — it appears in your menu bar (the camera icon). Pick a capture mode.

3

Grant Screen Recording when asked (once). That's it — capture away.

Signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple, so it installs without warnings.
