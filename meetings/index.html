MeetingRecorder

Record, transcribe & summarize any meeting — right on your Mac. Teams, Zoom, Meet, FaceTime… anything that makes sound.

⬇ Download for macOS

Requires macOS 15+ · Apple Silicon · Signed & notarized · Free

🎙️

Records both sides

Your mic and the meeting's audio as separate tracks — app-agnostic, no bots or plugins.

✍️

Accurate transcripts

OpenAI Whisper in the cloud, or fully on-device (Parakeet) — multilingual, incl. Czech.

🗣️

Who said what

On-device diarization splits participants into speakers you can rename.

📝

Meeting minutes

Summary, decisions and action items — via OpenAI or your local LM Studio / Ollama.

🔒

Private by design

Run the whole pipeline offline. Audio and notes stay in a folder on your Mac.

📄

Menu bar & Markdown

Lives in the menu bar, notifies when done, and exports clean Markdown.

Install in 3 steps

1

Open the downloaded DMG and drag MeetingRecorder into Applications.

2

Launch it — it appears in your menu bar (the ⓡ icon). Click Start Recording.

3

Grant Microphone and Screen & System Audio Recording when asked. That's it.

Signed with a Developer ID and notarized by Apple, so it installs without warnings.
