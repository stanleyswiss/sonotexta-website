Why people switch to VoiceFlow

🎙️

Instant Results

Your words appear the moment you speak them. Real-time cloud streaming with sub-second latency. 98+ languages detected automatically — no switching, no configuration.

🌍

Think in One Language, Write in Another

Dictate in your native tongue and VoiceFlow delivers the text in any of 24 languages. Per-contact tone profiles mean your wife gets casual Czech while your boss gets formal English.

👤

Smart Contact Detection

VoiceFlow reads who you're chatting with in iMessage, Teams, or Slack and automatically applies the right language and tone profile. No manual switching needed.

✨

AI That Polishes Your Words

Choose from 8 built-in enhancement presets — Professional, Bullet Points, Summary, Action Items, and more. Or write your own. Filler words gone, grammar fixed, tone perfected.

🌐

Globe Key & Hold to Talk

Use the Globe key as your dictation trigger. Two modes: tap to start/stop, or hold to talk and release to finish — just like a walkie-talkie. Custom keyboard shortcuts also supported.

🔒

Your Voice, Your Rules

Keep everything on your Mac with three on-device engines. Or use cloud streaming for maximum speed. You choose — no subscriptions required for local processing.

⚡

Invisible Until You Need It

Lives in your menu bar. No dock icon, no windows. A floating pill appears when you dictate, disappears when you're done. Works in every app — Messages, Slack, Obsidian, VS Code.

🔄

Automatic Updates

Always stay on the latest version. VoiceFlow checks for updates automatically and installs them with one click — no manual downloads needed.

🧠

6 AI Providers

OpenAI, Groq (free), Gemini (free), OpenRouter (200+ models), Ollama (fully local & private), and Ollama Cloud. Bring your own key or run completely offline.
